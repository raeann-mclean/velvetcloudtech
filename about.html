About Velvet Cloud

Your local partner for websites, IT, and everyday tech needs.

Velvet Cloud Tech exists for one reason: most local businesses can't justify a full-time IT person, but still deserve setups that are fast, secure, and actually looked after.

The mission

Make good technology feel simple and human, as it should be.

Big companies have whole teams keeping their cloud, security, and systems running. Small businesses usually get a patchwork of vendors, a few passwords on a sticky note, and a lot of crossed fingers.

I close that gap.I use the same core technologies trusted by larger organizations, including cloud infrastructure, automated backups, security best practices, and reliable monitoring - scaled to fit the needs and budgets of small businesses. Then I explain it in plain English, so you're never in the dark about your own tools!

The promise is simple: you focus on your business, I'll make sure the tech behind it always works.
